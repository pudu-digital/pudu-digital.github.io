Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use voice scrolling?

Open a script in the viewer and tap the play button. Speak naturally and the teleprompter will scroll automatically when it detects your voice. Pause speaking and the scrolling pauses too.

Why does the app need microphone access?

The microphone is used solely for voice-activated scrolling. Audio is processed on your device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or sent anywhere. You can review our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I change scroll speed?

In the viewer, tap the settings icon (gear) to open viewer settings. Use the speed slider to adjust how fast the text scrolls. The setting is saved automatically.

How do I enable mirror mode?

Open viewer settings (gear icon) and toggle mirror mode on. The text flips horizontally, which is useful when reading off a beam splitter or reflective glass.

The app isn't scrolling when I speak

Try these steps:

  • Make sure microphone permission is granted: go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone and ensure Voice Teleprompter is enabled.
  • Speak at a normal conversational volume — very quiet speech may not be detected.
  • Make sure you're not in a very noisy environment, which can interfere with voice detection.
  • Try closing and reopening the app.

How do I create, edit, or delete a script?

From the main screen, tap the + button to create a new script. Tap an existing script to open it for editing. To delete a script, swipe left on it in the script list and tap Delete.

Can I import scripts?

You can copy and paste text from other apps into a new or existing script. Direct file import is not currently supported.

How do I change the font size?

In the viewer, open settings (gear icon) and adjust the font size slider. The text size updates in real time so you can find the right size for your setup.
