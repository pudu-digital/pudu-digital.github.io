Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 23, 2026

This privacy policy applies to Voice Teleprompter PRO and Voice Teleprompter Lite (collectively, “the App”), published by Pudu Digital LLC (“we”, “us”, or “our”). It is provided in accordance with the Apple App Store privacy requirements.

Data Collection

Apple defines “collect” as transmitting data off the device in a way that allows the developer or third parties to access it. The App does not collect any data by this definition. All data accessed by the App remains on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Data accessed on-device only

The App accesses the following data, which is processed and stored entirely on your device:

Apple Data Type What the App accesses How it is used
Audio Data
(User Content)		 Microphone input via AVAudioRecorder Powers the voice-activated scrolling feature. Audio levels are analyzed in real time on-device. Audio is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.
Other User Content
(User Content)		 Script text entered by the user Stored locally on your device so you can create, edit, and reuse teleprompter scripts. Never transmitted off-device.

Per Apple’s guidance: “Data that is processed only on device is not ‘collected’ and does not need to be disclosed.” Neither of the above data types leaves your device.

Data types NOT accessed

The App does not access any of the following Apple data type categories:

  • Contact Info (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Health & Fitness
  • Financial Info
  • Location (precise or coarse)
  • Sensitive Info
  • Contacts
  • Browsing History
  • Search History
  • Identifiers (user ID, device ID)
  • Purchases
  • Usage Data (product interaction, advertising data)
  • Diagnostics (crash data, performance data)

Data Use Purposes

Since no data is collected (transmitted off-device), no data use purposes apply. For transparency:

Purpose Applies?
Third-Party AdvertisingNo

Data Linked to You

No data is linked to your identity. The App does not use accounts, logins, user IDs, device identifiers, or any other mechanism that could link data to you.

Tracking

The App does not track you. It does not link data collected from the App with data from other companies’ apps, websites, or offline properties for advertising or measurement purposes, nor does it share data with data brokers. The App does not use the AppTrackingTransparency framework because no tracking occurs.

Third-Party Code and SDKs

The App contains:

  • No third-party analytics SDKs
  • No advertising frameworks or ad networks
  • No third-party SDKs of any kind
  • No server-side components

The App is built entirely with Apple’s native iOS frameworks (UIKit, AVFoundation). No third-party code is included that could independently collect data.

Network Connections

The App does not make any network connections. It does not communicate with any external server, API, or service. The App functions entirely offline.

Data Retention and Deletion

Scripts are stored locally on your device until you delete them within the App. Microphone audio is processed in real time and immediately discarded—it is never saved. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data, including scripts and settings.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from any user, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is transmitted off-device, the App complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent regulations. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us.

Your Rights and Control

All data created by the App stays on your device and is fully under your control. Since we do not collect or store any data on our servers, there is no server-side data to request, export, or delete. You can delete your scripts at any time within the App, or uninstall the App to remove all data.

Security

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted over a network. Your scripts are stored using the device’s built-in local storage, protected by your device’s own security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
